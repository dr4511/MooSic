Welcome to MOOSIC


About Us

Sometimes it's easy to get too caught up in the grind of daily life, and your mental health can take a toll...take a second to relax and listen to some tunes with Gabe (picked just for you and your mood)! By taking a short quiz our site will tailor specific songs to you based on your answers to the questions.

Why MOOSIC?

Here at MOOSIC, we get the importance of mental health as well as the impact that good music can have. With our good friend Gabe, we're taking our love of music online to make space where users can take a step in prioritizing their mental health.

Meet Gabe!

Gabe is a chill and fun-loving cow who's always down for a good time. He loves listening to Pitbull and his favorite hobby is taking his suits to the dry-cleaners.
